Description

This is one of my favorites for when you are busy multi-tasking and have a little bit of time to wait. The recipie uses an Instant Pot and it is super quick to just throw everything in then step away to do something else. I would usually make this on a busy day working from home. I could quickly get everything cooking in the Instant Pot during a break, then get back to my work. Dinner would be waiting for me by the time I finished and there would be plenty of leftovers for lunch later in the week!
