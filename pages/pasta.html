Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Homepage

Description

This is one of my favorites for when you are busy multi-tasking and have a little bit of time to wait. The recipie uses an Instant Pot and it is super quick to just throw everything in then step away to do something else. I would usually make this on a busy day working from home. I could quickly get everything cooking in the Instant Pot during a break, then get back to my work. Dinner would be waiting for me by the time I finished and there would be plenty of leftovers for lunch later in the week!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 8 to 16 ounces dry pasta
  • 1 jar tomato-based pasta sauce (24 to 32 ounces, if you use more pasta, use more sauce)
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoons Italian herb seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 2 cups water (will be divided)

Steps

  1. Turn the Instant Pot to Saute on medium, set the timer for 10mins, then add the beef and sausage.
  2. Break up the meat using a spatula then add the Italian seasoning, garlic, chili flakes, pepper, 1 teaspoon of salt, and mix in.
  3. After the timer goes off, scrape the bottom of the pot to remove any stuck on bits, then add 1/2 cup of water and then the sauce. (Do not stir!)
  4. Add the pasta.
  5. Add the remaining water, then the remaining salt.
  6. Cook on HIGH for 9 minutes
  7. After pressure has been released, stir well and serve!
