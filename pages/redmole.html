Mole Rojo

Homepage

Description

Another favorite, this recipie takes a little more time due to prep, but is big on flavor. The recipe also uses an Instant Pot, so you can throw everything in and go do something else while it cooks. This recipe also yields plenty for leftovers, so you can set some aside for another day's lunch or dinner! I usually have this dish alongside some corn tortillas or naan.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of chicken breast
  • 1 jar of mole rojo sauce (12 ounces, I usually buy Ya Oaxaca brand)
  • 1 yellow onion (small, sliced)
  • 3 poblano peppers (sliced)
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 can whole kernel corn
  • 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup water (will be divided)

Steps

  1. Add the chicken to the Instant Pot.
  2. Add the salt, pepper, and 1/2 cup of water.
  3. Zest the lime and add to the pot.
  4. Quarter the lime and set aside for serving.
  5. Add the onion, poblano peppers, garlic, and corn.
  6. Add the mole rojo sauce
  7. Add the remaining water to the sauce jar and shake.
  8. Add the water/sauce mix to the pot.
  9. Pressure cook on HIGH for 12 minutes.
  10. After the presure is released, turn to Saute mode on Medium and set timer for 5 minutes.
  11. The pot will come to a boil, stir to break up the chicken breast into shreds.
  12. After the timer goes off, give it some time to cool then serve!
