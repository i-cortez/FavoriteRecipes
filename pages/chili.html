Delicious Chili

Homepage

Description

This chili recipe is my most favorite. It is perfect for a cold winter's day and will leave you feeling cozy and well satisfied. It is made with an Instant Pot, which makes it super simple. Just toss everything in and let the Instant Pot work its magic. The recipe is based on and nearly identical to Sara Welch's Instant Pot Chili recipe (all credit goes to Sara), found on Dinner at the Zoo. However, I have made some adjustments. I use no olive oil (the meat has plenty of grease for my taste), substitute ground beef for ground bison, use beef bone broth instead of plain beef broth (a little more expensive but worth it IMO), add in a few diced jalapenos, and some Truff brand hot sauce. I usually serve alongside some cornbread muffins. So good together!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground bison (90% lean)
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 to 3 diced jalapenos (depending on desired heat level)
  • 1 + 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 28 ounce can of diced tomatoes (do not drain)
  • 3 cans of tomato sauce (8 ounces each)
  • 3/4 cup of beef bone broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground pepper
  • 2 cans of beans (15 ounces each, drained and rinsed, I prefer black, pinto, kidney mix)
  • 2 tablespoons Truff brand black truffle hot sauce
  • Toppings such as shredded cheese, sour cream, etc... (I like to put plain Icelandic Skyr for extra protien!)

Steps

  1. Turn your Instant Pot to Saute on medium.
  2. Add the meat and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spatula.
  3. Add 1 teaspoon of salt and 1 teaspoon of pepper to the meat, and stir.
  4. Add the onion, garlic, jalapenos, and cook for at most a few more minutes.
  5. Add the broth to the pot.
  6. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, cocoa powder, sugar, remaining salt, remaining pepper, hot sauce, and beans to the pot. (Do not stir! Leave as much liquid as possible at the bottom of the pot to avoid BURN message!)
  7. Turn the Instant Pot to the Pressure Cook setting and place the lid on the pot.
  8. Cook on HIGH for 20 minutes.
  9. Release the pressure either manually or allow it to release naturally.
  10. Stir well and serve!
